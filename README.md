Overview

This project analyzes sales performance using a dataset of 5,001 records.
The objective was to transform raw data into actionable insights and build a dashboard to support business decision-making.

⸻

Data Preparation

Cleaned dataset and removed duplicates to ensure data accuracy
Validated Order ID integrity to prevent double-counting
Created new features:
Discount Range using logical grouping (IFS)
Month column for time-based trend analysis

⸻

Analysis Performed

Sales and Profit by Region
Sales and Profit by Category
Profit by Discount Range
Monthly Sales Trend

⸻

Key Insights

The South region generates the highest sales and profit, indicating strong market performance
Lower discount levels are associated with higher profitability, suggesting that aggressive discounting reduces margins
Sales remain relatively stable over time, with periodic spikes that may be driven by seasonal or promotional factors

⸻

Business Implications

Reducing high discount usage could improve overall profitability
The South region represents a strong revenue driver and may benefit from further investment
Identifying causes of sales spikes can help optimize future sales strategies
